  <h3>Overview</h3>
  <p>
    As part of a team project, I developed machine learning models to predict movie box office performance 
    and identify key drivers of franchise success using a 1M-record TMDb dataset.
  </p>

  <h3>My Contributions</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Cleaned and preprocessed large-scale movie data, handling missing values and removing sparse features</li>
    <li>Engineered features including release date transformations and multi-label genre encoding</li>
    <li>Built and evaluated Linear Regression, Random Forest, and Logistic Regression models</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Results</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Achieved up to <strong>R² = 0.75</strong> for revenue prediction</li>
    <li>Reached <strong>72% accuracy</strong> in classifying box office success</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Tools & Technologies</h3>

    <li>Python (pandas, scikit-learn)</li>
