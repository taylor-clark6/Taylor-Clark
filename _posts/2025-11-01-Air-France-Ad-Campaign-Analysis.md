<h2>Air France Ad Campaign Analysis</h2>

  <h3>Overview</h3>
  <p>
    This project analyzes digital advertising campaign performance for Air France using Excel and Python.
    The goal was to evaluate profitability, optimize marketing efficiency, and provide data-driven insights
    to improve return on investment.
  </p>

  <h3>My Contributions</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Modeled campaign performance with a 10% cost reduction and 5% revenue increase</li>
    <li>Calculated key metrics including margin, cost per booking, and cost/revenue variance</li>
    <li>Built a P&amp;L statement using pivot tables with conditional formatting for clear insights</li>
    <li>Identified top 10 revenue-generating campaigns using pivot analysis</li>
    <li>Created visualizations for ROAS and revenue vs. cost per booking by publisher</li>
    <li>Designed a scenario analysis model to determine CTR required to increase profit by 20%</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Key Insights</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Highlighted high-performing campaigns driving the majority of revenue</li>
    <li>Identified inefficiencies in cost per booking across publishers</li>
    <li>Showed that increasing CTR alone does not always lead to higher profitability</li>
    <li>Provided a clear framework for optimizing marketing spend and performance</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Tools & Technologies</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Conditional Formatting)</li>
    <li>Python (data analysis)</li>
    <li>Financial modeling & scenario analysis</li>
  </ul>
